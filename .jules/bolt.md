## 2024-05-15 - Performance optimization for hybrid triggers loop **Erkenntnis:** In `timeline_v2/ui.rs`, a linear scan (`O(N)`) over `blocks` was being performed on every iteration of a loop checking for non-triggered active blocks. **Aktion:** Hoisted the invariant `current_is_active` calculation outside the loop and cached `hybrid_active_triggers` as a `HashSet` before the loop using turbofish syntax to satisfy automated review constraints.
